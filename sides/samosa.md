# Samosa

## Ingredients

- Flour
- Water
- Oil
- Potato
- Salt
- Chillies


## Instructions

- Knead the flour, water and oil to make a dough
- Boil the potatoes
- Fry the boiled potatoes with salt and chillies
- Roll the dough to create triangles
- Fill the dough with potato stuffing
- Deep fry in oil
- Serve hot!
